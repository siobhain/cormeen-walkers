Who we are & How to find us?

If you are looking to walk on quiet country roads, some with grass growing in the middle then you have come to the right place. We surely have a walking group to meet your needs, from our Power Walkers who meet twice a week (Mon & Fri), to our Buggy Walkers for adults with baby strollers (Wed) and finally our Family Walkers who take a leisurely walk on Saturdays!

All walks start at 10AM from the car park of Cormeen Sports Complex (EIRCODE A82 FT62) & take approx 1 hour.

Did you know Cormeen Sports Complex is in North County Meath close to the Cavan border, It is equidistant to the towns of Kells, Kingscourt & Bailieborough.
