About Cormeen Walkers

Cormeen is a rural community in North County Meath close to the Cavan border, If you are looking to walk on quiet country lanes with grass growing in the middle of some then you have come to the right place. We surely have a walking group to meet your needs.


  • Power Walkers: Monday & Friday
  • Buggy Walkers: Wednesday & baby strollers/wheelchairs welcome
  • Family Walkers: Saturday

All walks take approx one hour and start at 10AM from the car park of Cormeen Sports Complex, the eircode is A82 FT62 & its pinned on our google maps - see the next 2 panels. Best of all is that this is totally FREE just turn up at 10am and enjoy the company and the countyside!

About Cormeen Sports Complex

Cormeen Sports Complex is nestled in beautiful Meath countryside on the borderline of Moynalty and Kilmainhamwood parishes. There is a large carpark which can accomodate up to 48 cars. Tea/Coffee and toilet facilities are available onsite. It is equidistant from the towns of Kells Kingscourt and Bailieborough.
