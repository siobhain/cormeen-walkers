About Cormeen Walkers


Cormeen is a rural community in North County Meath close to the Cavan border, If you are looking to walk on quiet country lanes with grass growing in the middle of some then you have come to the right place. We surely have a walking group to meet your needs.


  • Power Walkers: Monday & Friday
  • Buggy Walkers: Wednesday
  • Family Walkers: Saturday

All walks take approx one hour and start at 10AM from the car park of Cormeen Sports Complex, the eircode is A82 FT62 & its pinned on our google maps panel. Best of all is that this is totally FREE ...just turn up at 10am and enjoy the Company, the Countyside & Cormeen!

About Cormeen Sports Complex


Cormeen Sports Complex is nestled in the beautiful Meath countryside on the borderlines of Moynalty and Kilmainhamwood parishes. There is a large carpark which can accomodate up to 48 cars. Tea/coffee & toilet facilities are available onsite. It is equidistant from the towns of Kells, Kingscourt and Bailieborough, so get those walking boots on and come out to the REAL sticks!



About Directions


Cormeen Sports Complex is 8Km from Moynalty on the R164 road out of the village towards Kingscourt. Search via the eircode A82 FT62 on Eircode Finder or see it pinned here on google maps.
