About Cormeen Walkers


Cormeen Walkers is a term we use to describe the walking groups who start their walk from Cormeen Sports Complex. There are 3 groups in all from the Power Walkers who meet twice a week, to our Buggy Walkers who walk with their baby buggies and finally to our Family Walkers where young and old can walk topgether at a more leisurely pace. The meeting location of Cormeen Sports Complex is nestled within a rural community in North County Meath close to the Cavan border. If you are looking to walk on quiet country lanes with grass growing in the middle of some then you have come to the right place. We hope to have a walking group to meet your needs.

  • Power Walkers: Mon & Fri
  • Buggy Walkers: Wednesday
  • Family Walkers: Saturday

All walks take approx one hour and start at 10AM from the car park of Cormeen Sports Complex, the eircode is A82 FT62 & its pinned on our google maps panel. Best of all is that this is totally FREE ...just show up at 10am and enjoy the Company, the Countyside & Cormeen!

Cormeen Sports Complex


Cormeen Sports Complex is nestled in the beautiful Meath countryside on the borderlines of Moynalty and Kilmainhamwood parishes. There is a large carpark which can accomodate up to 48 cars. Tea/coffee & toilet facilities are available onsite. It is equidistant from the towns of Kells, Kingscourt and Bailieborough, so get those walking boots on and come out to the REAL sticks!



Driving Directions


Cormeen Sports Complex is 8Km from Moynalty on the R164 road out of the village towards Kingscourt. To get online directions search via the eircode A82 FT62 or the Sports Complex is pinned here for you on google maps.
